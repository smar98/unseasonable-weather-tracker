Methodology Screening rules
  • Each day is compared with the same day-of-year +/- 7 days in 1991-2020.
  • Heat, cold, precipitation, and snowfall are scored separately before any ranking.
  • The score is a review queue, not climate attribution, impact, or risk.

Interpretation boundary

Weather anomaly, not attribution

Each day is compared with the same day-of-year window in the 1991-2020 baseline. The score ranks candidates for review. Public claims should be validated against official station data, satellite snow cover, or other independent evidence.
